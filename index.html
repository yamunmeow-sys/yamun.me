About Yamun

I am an anthropology and education researcher, educational innovator, and activist. My work bridges research and activism, guided by a belief in an anthropology of activism.

My research interests are: traditions and modernities, alternative pedagogies, educational cultures, and ethnographic methods.

Through this blog, I hope to share my art, experiences, and ideas, and connect with other like-minded people.
